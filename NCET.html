3.) My Favorite Recipe : Briyani

Steps for the recipe.

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices, and herbs for 1 hour.
  3. Fry onions till golden brown and set aside.
  4. Cook the marinated chicken till tender.
  5. Boil the rice till it's 70% cooked.
  6. Layer chicken and rice, topping with fried onions and saffron milk.
  7. Cover and cook on low flame (dum) for 20 minutes.
  8. Serve hot with raita or salan.

List of Ingredients.

  • Basmati Rice
  • Chicken
  • Yogurt
  • Onions
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Spices (turmeric, chili powder, garam masala)
  • Mint & Coriander Leaves
  • Saffron soaked in warm milk
  • Ghee or oil
  • Salt

Cooking terms.

Marinate
Letting the meat or veggies sit in a mix of yogurt, spices, or sauces so they taste better and get softer.
Dum
A way of cooking where the pot is closed tightly and cooked slowly on low heat so all the flavors blend well.
Saute
Quickly frying something (like onions or veggies) in a little oil on medium or high heat.
Biryani
A yummy rice dish made with layers of spiced meat or vegetables and basmati rice, all cooked together.
